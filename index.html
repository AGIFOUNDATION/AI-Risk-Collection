AI Risk Collection (AIRC)

Objective

AIRC is dedicated to collecting, curating, and categorizing real-world examples of AI risks that emerge during research and deployment...

Key Goals

  • Data Collection & Curation

    • Identify and collect detailed AI risk examples from real-world applications across diverse sectors (e.g., healthcare, finance, autonomous systems).

  • Risk Categorization

    • Systematically categorize risks based on the nature of failure, harm, or unintended outcomes.

  • Risk Prediction & Prevention

    • Create a repository of case studies that can help developers and regulators foresee potential risks during AI system development and deployment.

  • Community Collaboration

    • Encourage contributions from researchers, engineers, policymakers, and stakeholders for a robust, multi-disciplinary understanding of AI risks.

  • Practical Solutions & Best Practices

    • Develop guidelines for the safe deployment of AI systems, addressing specific risks based on collected examples.
